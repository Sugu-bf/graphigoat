The Shortlist Is Changing

Buyers no longer only search. They ask AI to compare, recommend, and eliminate.

"Which LED driver matches the low-profile spec? Who carries fire-rated downlights with clean submittals?"

AI-assisted search increasingly summarizes, compares, and recommends products using information it can discover and interpret. When product pages, technical documents, structured data, and submittal resources are difficult to crawl or understand, a brand may be less likely to appear in those answers.
